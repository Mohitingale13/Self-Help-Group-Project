I want to extend my existing Self Help Group (SHG) management system by adding payment mode selection, QR payment support, and improved statement tracking.
Currently, the system has three roles:
•	President
•	Treasurer
•	Member
Members make monthly payments and can also request loans.
I want to implement the following features.
________________________________________
1. Monthly Payment Mode Selection
When a Member makes their monthly payment, they should be able to choose the payment mode:
•	Cash
•	Online
Behavior
•	If the member selects Cash, the payment is recorded normally.
•	If the member selects Online, the system should display the Treasurer’s QR code so the member can scan and complete the payment.
________________________________________
2. Treasurer QR Code Upload
The Treasurer should have the ability to upload their QR code.
Requirements:
•	Provide an upload option in the Treasurer dashboard to upload a QR image.
•	The uploaded QR code should be stored in the system/database.
•	When a Member selects Online Payment, the Treasurer’s uploaded QR code should automatically appear on the member’s screen.
________________________________________
3. Payment Verification by Treasurer
For online payments, the Treasurer must verify that the payment has actually been completed.
Workflow:
1.	Member selects Online Payment and makes payment via QR.
2.	The payment status becomes "Pending Treasurer Verification".
3.	The Treasurer checks whether the payment was received.
4.	Treasurer then:
o	Approves payment → status becomes "Payment Confirmed"
o	Rejects payment → status becomes "Payment Not Received"
________________________________________
4. Loan Approval Tracking
The system already has loan approval by Treasurer and President.
Enhance this by recording timestamps:
•	When the Treasurer approves the loan
•	When the President gives final approval
These timestamps should be stored in the database.
________________________________________
5. Statement Generator PDF Improvements
The statement generator PDF should include detailed information such as:
Payment Details
•	Payment amount
•	Payment date
•	Payment mode (Cash or Online)
•	Payment status
•	Treasurer verification status
Loan Details
•	Loan amount
•	Loan request date
•	Treasurer approval date
•	President approval date
•	Loan status
This information should be clearly structured in the generated PDF statement.
________________________________________
6. Summary of Features to Implement
1.	Payment mode selection (Cash / Online).
2.	Treasurer QR code upload and storage.
3.	Display Treasurer QR to members for online payment.
4.	Treasurer verification step for online payments.
5.	Store timestamps for Treasurer and President loan approvals.
6.	Improve PDF statement generator to include:
o	Payment mode
o	Verification status
o	Approval timestamps.
Please implement this in a clean, modular way so the system remains scalable and maintainable.
